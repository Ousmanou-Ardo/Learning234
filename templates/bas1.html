This club aims at providing students with an environment where they can build, practice and unleash their diplomatic and negotiation potentials through the simulation of the OIC Council of Foreign ministers

What we are aiming for

The Model OIC is an academic simulation of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC), which aims at educating participants about current trends in theory and practice of International relations, effective communication and multilateral diplomacy. 

Our Motivation

The current position and potential future of the Ummah is the biggest motivating factor for us.

What We Offer?

We offer all Muslim youth the opportunity to be united for a fairer world. 

To build a fairer world by organizing the Muslim youth within the framework of diplomacy.
